Pacemaker, 2021
Pacemaker explores the jolting flux of our perceptions of time as our IRL pace accelerates to catch up to the immediacy offered by life in URL. It was written while reflecting on these contrasting worlds.

Pacemaker echoes a fragmented pace of life, phasing between rapid convenience and stay-at-home slowness, with a quivering jingle and erratic percussive injections.

Made by Cardiff-based electronic artist and producer Jack of the Suburbs.
