route guide to Cwm Pwca waterfalls


A short journey from the ironworks car park to Cwm Pwca falls, alongside a steep-sided gorge in the Clydach Gorge. Follow the images below in order - the route is a guide only, please explore at your own discretion and risk.


Please note, this route includes uneven terrain, moderate inclines, and potential obstacles, and may not be accessible for wheelchair users.


Images were taken at the start of summer 2026, different seasonal versions of the site will become available over the next year.
