openDAW

Music making in a DAW is often an individual activity, and therefore an isolating experience. openDAW is about encouraging the sharing and transparency of composition and production processes within DAWs.

I'm doing my sharing here, but plan to organise meets where artists can get together and share in realtime, allowing for questions to guide the unpacking of personal processes and unique, habitual strategies.
